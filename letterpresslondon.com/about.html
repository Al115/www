About

LETTERpRESS LONDON is a small press located in Hatfield, Hertfordshire, specialising in custom letterpress products.

Our Head Printress, Tomiko, started letterpressing in Chicago in 2004, at the Columbia College Center for Book and Paper Arts - one six-week course, and she was hooked. In 2007, Tomiko moved North and continued her printing at the Minneapolis Center for Book Arts, followed by a relocation to Hertfordshire in the UK in 2010. Tomiko has experience working with a wide range of presses, including her personal Vandercook SP-15 and Adana Eight-Five.

LETTERpRESS LONDON would love to help you come up with the perfect invitation, announcement, stationary... or whatever you’d like. Please feel free to contact Tomiko to discuss your requirements.
